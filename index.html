Riga Water Pumps is a fast-growing automotive parts brand committed to precision engineering and long-lasting performance. We design every pump to meet exact OEM tolerances — because in a cooling system, precision isn't optional.

Manufactured to OEM standards, our pumps are trusted by dealers and distributors across India for reliability in every condition — from Ladakh's altitude to Chennai's heat.

Distributed exclusively through Continental Agencies, Riga combines the quality standards of global OEM suppliers with competitive pricing tailored for the Indian market. Our growing network ensures fast availability from Jammu to Kanyakumari.
